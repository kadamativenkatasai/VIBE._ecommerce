FAQ'S Page

Home > FAQ'S

When will my order ship?

Typically, orders placed before 12 pm EST—Monday through Friday (excluding holidays)—will ship the same day. If your order is placed after 12 pm EST on Friday, it will be processed the following Monday.

How do I return an item?

For most items purchased through the vibe. Shop, you can use our self-service return portal based on your region, check out this article for more info.

How do I exchange an item?

Complete our return process and ship your item back to us—then order the new item. We will process your return as soon as possible after receiving the returned item, so hopefully it's not too much of an inconvenience.

What countries do you ship to?

We currently ship to the United States, United Kingdom, and European Union.

Can I cancel my order?

We can't cancel orders, but you can return an item after you've received it. For more info on returning an order, check out this article.

How do I know what size to buy?

To help you pick the right size, we've included a sizing chart directly on the product page for most of our apparel items.
